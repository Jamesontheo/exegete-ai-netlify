About Exegete.AI

What is Exegete.AI?

Exegete.AI is a Scripture-first Reformed theology assistant designed to help you explore biblical truth through the lens of Reformed doctrine. Every response begins with Scripture and is grounded in God's Word.

How to Use

  • Ask Questions: Type any theological or biblical question
  • Browse Topics: Use the sidebar to explore Reformed doctrines
  • Reference Scripture: Click on Bible books or search for verses
  • Explore Theologians: Learn what Reformed theologians taught
  • Toggle Theme: Switch between light and dark modes

Reformed Framework

All responses are grounded in:

  • Scripture as the ultimate authority (Sola Scriptura)
  • The Doctrines of Grace (TULIP)
  • Covenant Theology
  • Westminster Standards
  • Historic Reformed Confessions

Featured Theologians

Responses draw from the wisdom of Reformed theologians including John Calvin, Martin Luther, Augustine, R.C. Sproul, John Piper, Wayne Grudem, Tim Keller, and D.A. Carson.
